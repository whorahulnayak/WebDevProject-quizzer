Rules

Alright so here are some rules you gotta follow

  1. Only individual entries are eligible. No team participation.
  2. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
  3. The questions shall be in the form of multiple choice.
  4. Participant found to involve in any sort of malpractice shall be disqualified immediately and Score and Level will be reset to 0.
  5. Participant can select one of many quiz categories on our site which will instantly begin the quiz.
  6. A total of 5 questions will be asked per category after which the score will be calculated.
  7. Each correct answer fetches 10 points.
  8. No negative marking.
  9. The leaderboard page will feature the top 10 participants who scored the highest in all the quizzes combined.
  10. Participants can contact us incase they face any technical difficulties.
  11. HAPPY QUIZZING!!
